Description

Lightsabers are the weapon of Jedi Knights, not as clumsy or random as a blaster but an elegant weapon for a more civilized age.

Equipment

  • High Temperature Kiln
  • Hydrospanner
  • Soldering Iron
  • Electroplating Bath
  • Suspension Grip
  • Pliers
  • Microwelder

Instructions

  1. Tune focusing and kyber crystals inside the kiln, while using the Force to find the correct frequency of the crystals. Allow the kiln to resolve microstructures for 24 more standard hours.
  2. During the curing process for the crystals, allow the electrum plating to anneal in the electroplating bath.
  3. Use soldering iron to bridge emitter fixtures and inhibitor, handling connector terminals with pliers or utilizing the Force.
  4. Prepare the hilt outer casing by fastening durasteel cylinder in the suspension grip, while using the hydrospanner to merge the plasteel grip and belt clip together.
  5. Use the force to bring all of the components together.
